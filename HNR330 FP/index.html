Introduction to Green Burial Movement

Death is inescapable. Try as we might, we have no choice in our own mortality. But we can choose what happens to us after we die, and this decision is one of the most important you’ll ever make. While conventional funerals involving environmentally harmful chemicals and materials have become the norm, the Green Burial movement highlights the need for a return to our tradition of being buried with the Earth, not locked off from it. Conventional burials introduce harmful contaminants into the Earth, while green burials allow us to give back to the land we’ve grown on, as our bodies’ give the soil nutrients that allow other species to thrive. By reclaiming our connection to the Earth, we can truly contribute to the cycle of life that supports us all.
