Conventional Burial

When we say conventional burial we mean a burial that you typically see and think about when you think of a burial. One where there is a concrete vault and the body is buried in a casket. One downside of conventional burials is that it takes up a lot of space. A standard grave is 2 and a half feet wide by 8 feet long. This does not include the measurements of the concrete vault or lining that goes into the grave.
